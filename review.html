A mythic and emotionally charged hero’s journey, Dune tells the story of Paul Atreides, a brilliant and gifted young man born into a great destiny beyond his understanding,
who must travel to the most dangerous planet in the universe to ensure the future of his family
and his people. As malevolent forces explode into conflict over the planet’s exclusive supply of the most precious resource in
existence—a commodity capable of unlocking humanity’s greatest potential—only those who can conquer their fear will survive

This Dune dwarfs most contemporary sci-fi in its scope and execution, ably juggling multiple characters and settings so that it matches the sprawling drama of the original tome.
